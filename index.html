CheongJu, Korea jhhwghg9911@chungbuk.ac.kr GitHub

I am a Ph.D. student at Chungbuk National University, under the supervision of Young-Seob Jung. My research interests lie in Natural Language Processing and Machine Learning, with a particular focus on Large Language Models (LLMs). I study how LLMs learn from and utilize large-scale text corpora, aiming to better understand and advance their capabilities. I received my B.S. and M.S. degrees in Computer Engineering from Chungbuk National University, where I was also advised by Young-Seob Jung.
